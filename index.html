How We Work

Our Collaborative Process

01
Consultation & Listening

Your journey begins with an in-depth conversation. We listen to your concerns, goals, and dental history — no rushing, no assumptions. You set the agenda.

02
Comprehensive Examination

Digital X-rays, 3D scans, and clinical assessment give us a complete picture of your oral health. We identify issues before they become costly problems.

03
Tailored Treatment Plan

Together, we design a personalised treatment plan — with clear timelines, transparent pricing, and step-by-step explanations. You approve every decision.

04
Precision Treatment

Using state-of-the-art technology and proven techniques, Dr. MIR delivers each stage of treatment with meticulous care and attention to detail.

05
Aftercare & Maintenance

Our relationship doesn't end at treatment. Regular check-ins, tailored hygiene advice, and long-term monitoring protect your investment for years to come.
